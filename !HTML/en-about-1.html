Our perfomance

Joint Stock Company Severalmaz is located in Northwestern part of Russia, in Arkhangelsk region, and is one of the youngest diamond extraction companies in Russia.

Severalmaz was found according to the decree of Russian Federation Government of 20 March 1992 “On behalf of the industrial development of M.V. Lomonosov diamond deposits in Arkhangelsk region and the creation of production facilities for extraction, sorting, cutting diamonds and manufacturing of diamond tools”.

M.V. Lomonosov diamond deposit has six kimberlite pipes: Arkhangelskaya, of Karpinskiy-1, of Karpinsky-2, Pionerskaya, Pomorskaya, and of Lomonosov. JSC Severalmaz was registered in Arkhangelsk on 27 of August 1992. Company’s main shareholder is JSC ALROSA.

Industrial diamond extraction conducted from tubes "Arkhangelskaya" and of Karpinskiy-1. Processing plant can process up to 4 million tons of ore per year

Our History
JSC Severalmaz was registered in Arkhangelsk on 27 of August 1992. Industrial diamond mining started June 28, 2005, with the commissioning of the first line of the Lomonosov ore mining and processing complex with capacity of 1 million tons of ore per year. On March 14, 2014, Severalmaz commissioned the second line of processing plant, increasing capacity from 1 to 4 million tons of ore per year.

Our Activity

JSC Severalmaz primary activity is extraction and processing of diamond ore up to obtaining pure natural diamonds.

The Company has core industrial divisions engaged in extraction, transportation, and processing of raw materials, geological prospecting, as well as auxiliary workshops, ensures preparation of primary production and living conditions for workers.

Operations

The Supreme governing body of JSC Severalmaz is General Meeting of Shareholders; they make decisions concerning Severalmaz’s primary activities. The Board of directors carries out general management of JSC Severalmaz, they have competency to determine priority activities for the company. CEO and directorate manage the day-to-day activities of the company and are accountable to the Board of directors and General Meeting of Shareholders.

Board of Directors

The Board of directors carries out general management of JSC Severalmaz. The Board of directors acts in accordance with the legislation of the Russian Federation, the Charter of JSC Severlamaz, and Regulations on the Board of Directors of JSC Severalmaz.

The Board of Directors competence includes resolving general issues related to the management of the Company and determining priority areas for the Company's activities and development. In particular, the Board of Directors solves such issues as: Convocation of the annual and extraordinary general meeting of shareholders and approval of the agenda for the meeting. Recommendation on the amount of dividend per share and its payment procedure Approval of major transactions and interested party transactions in cases stipulated by the Charter of JSC Severalmaz and Russian legislation.

A complete list of issues related to the competence of the general meeting of shareholders is set out in the Charter of PJSC Severalmaz (paragraph 14.2).
